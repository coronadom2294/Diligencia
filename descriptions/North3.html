North Hypothesis 3

Separate Room

As seen in this hypothesis, we have decided to separate the particular part from the rest of the building. This approach was devised with the understanding that a metallurgical installation would produce smoke, and thus it did not seem to be ideal to have such an inconvenient structure directly attached to the main building. Thus the idea of a separate metal shop came to mind; and based on that idea we developed a hypothesis that would illustrate this.
