What Can Be Seen Today?

Pictured below is an ariel view of the location of the "Palace" (B.F8/9); you may note that certain parts still need to be excavated. To the left a model has been created in order to give a visual representation of what is known from the excavated area of the site.

B.F8/9 "Palace" Floor Plan

Presented is a model created based on the master floor plan from the Princeton Excavation of the area B.F8/9; the model was intended for the purpose of providing a visual representation of the known, the unknown, and the uncertain. Furthermore, in order to provide an interpretation for areas of B.F8/9 that are unknown or questionable, elements of what is known have been taken into account in order to help make a prediction on how certain parts of B.F8/9 might have looked like--thus our hypothesis represent an interpretation of what we think could have been possible, keep in mind that these are hypothetical.
