Black-on-Red II(IV) Ware

Height: .195 m, Diameter: .285 m

This large cup was glued together and filled in with plaster. Two stripes of concentric circles fill the area between and above the two small handles. Thick and thin stripes line the rest of the body and foot. The inside is decorated with two stripes vertically alone the rim and groups of thinner stripes farther down the body.
