Second Story Hypothesis 1

Once again, following the understanding that this is hypothetical, and that we are correlating the widths of the walls to a predicted second story, we also developed this visual representation. As noticed, this particular hypothesis shows the thick wall between the two courtyards to be the same height as the second story. Thus, although the second story completely circulates the outer-courtyard, humans would not be able to go directly from the northwest to the northeast. The reason we did this was to gain a better insight of how the space could be used with minor differences.
