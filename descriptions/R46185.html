Chian Amphora: R46185/PO1580 (Princeton Cyprus Expedition)

Preserved Height: .7 m

This chian amphora is made of clay and was reconstructed from shards. It has a white surface that is decorated by the painted red lines. The amphora was discovered on August 4, 2003 in the cistern of the palace, and experts have dated it back to the third quarter of the sixth century.
