Cypoit transport Amphora: R50600/PO2031 (Gloria Lee)

This white, transport amphora features vertical handles and yellow clay. Its pieces were found on August 4, 2003 in the cistern from area b.f8/9. In 2007, the discovered pieces were joined with other pieces from the artifact R50599 in order to form a visual representation of a V transport amphora.
