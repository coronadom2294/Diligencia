Bichrome Amphora: R50546/PO2021 (Princeton Cyprus Expedition)

Preservd Height: .16 m

This ceramic bichrome amphora was found on August 5, 2003 in the cistern. A checkerboard pattern aligns the upper neck, while black and red stripes are found underneath. Unfortunately, only the neck remains of this precious artifact.

Trench B.F9:b06.2003, 17, 5
