Bichrome Amphora: R46632/PO1586 (Princeton Cyprus Expedition)

Preserved Height: .24 m, Width: .57 m

The top of this terracotta bichrome amphora was found on August 5, 2003 in the cistern. The neck pieces are of East Grecian style. Body fragments were also found and reconstructed; but what is interesting about this specific artifact is that the top was not attached to the body.

Trench B.F9:b08:2003, 17, pass 5, 6, 7
