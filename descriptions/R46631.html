Clazomenian Amphora: R46631/PO1585 (Nassos Papalexandrou)

Preserved Height: .42 m

This Clazomenian amphora was reconstructed from terracotta fragments found on August 5, 2003 in the cistern. Based on the remains, it's known that the neck was broken off in antiquity. Furthermore, this type of amphora is typical of the third quarter of the sixth century BC.

Find Book: 2003/1: 20

Trench Book: 2003/1, pg 134
